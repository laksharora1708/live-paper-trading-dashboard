Avoid Longs During Fear

Extreme Fear had an 86.8% win rate yet still lost money overall. Small wins were wiped out by catastrophic losses of -$342 mean PnL. High win rates don't guarantee profitability.

During Extreme Greed, SELL trades made $2.62M vs BUY trades losing -$206K. This counterintuitive signal suggests top traders systematically sold into strength — a classic "sell the euphoria" strategy.
